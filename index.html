About 'hello world'



'Hello world' is an interactive and personalized travel journal. With it, you can map out all the countries and places you have visited, as well as all the memories you have collected in the form of photographs. Go ahead - shade in your countries, add your prized photographs and know that since it's on the internet, it will live forever.

This project was the brainchild of Diksha Radhakrishnan. This is her 'hello world'. Click on the United Kingdom and discover her travel journal.
